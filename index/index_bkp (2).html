Our Journey

Founded on the principles of engineering excellence, Industrial Automation Solution has grown into a trusted partner for global manufacturers. Our approach combines rigorous technical standards with innovative problem-solving.

Our Core Solutions

Machine Automation

High-speed logic and motion control for standalone machinery. We specialize in SPM (Special Purpose Machines) logic using high-end PLCs and Servos for precision manufacturing.

Plant & Factory Automation

End-to-end integration of factory floors. We connect multiple production lines to a centralized control system, allowing for seamless data flow and synchronization.

Process Automation

Specialized solutions for continuous process industries like Chemical, Pharma, and Water Treatment. We handle PID loops, flow control, and safety instrumented systems (SIS).

Electrical Control Panels

Custom design and fabrication of CP, MCC, PCC, and VFD panels. All panels are built to international standards with rigorous testing before dispatch.

Component Repair

Component-level repair services for PLCs, HMIs, and VFDs. We help reduce costs by reviving your existing hardware with certified testing.

24/7 Support & AMC

Annual Maintenance Contracts (AMC) and emergency on-site support to ensure zero production loss. We are your long-term reliability partners.
