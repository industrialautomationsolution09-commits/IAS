Our Core Solutions

Machine Automation & SPMs

We deliver high-speed logic and motion control for standalone machinery, specializing in Special Purpose Machines (SPM) using high-end PLCs and Servos for precision manufacturing.

Our services include comprehensive retrofitting, modification, and up-gradation of legacy machines to improve cycle times and reliability. We also integrate advanced Material Handling systems and Poka-Yoke (error-proofing) logic to eliminate defects and optimize industrial productivity.

Plant & Factory Automation

We offer end-to-end integration of factory floors, connecting multiple production lines to a centralized SCADA control system for seamless synchronization.

Our solutions focus on high-speed industrial Networking and secure Data Logging, ensuring every critical parameter is recorded. We design customized Reporting dashboards that allow plant managers to monitor OEE (Overall Equipment Effectiveness) and performance metrics in real-time from any location.

Process Automation & Traceability

We deliver high-performance solutions for continuous process industries, specializing in PID loop tuning, flow control, and Safety Instrumented Systems (SIS) for the Chemical and Pharma sectors.

Our expertise covers automated Mixing and Batching as well as specialized Water Treatment plant logic. To ensure end-to-end traceability, we implement integrated Barcode Scanning, Printing, and Labeling systems. Combined with robust Material Handling, we ensure your production line meets the highest standards of safety and efficiency.

Electrical Control Panels

We specialize in the custom design and high-quality fabrication of APFC, AMF, MCC, PLC, and VFD panels, tailored to meet your specific project requirements.

Every system we build adheres to international engineering standards. Our production process includes rigorous high-voltage testing, point-to-point continuity checks, and internal safety audits. Whether you need a centralized Motor Control Center (MCC) or a complex PLC-based control desk, our panels provide the precision and protection your operations demand.

Component Repair & Troubleshooting

We offer expert component-level repair for critical hardware including PLCs, VFDs, HMIs, and Servo Drives. Our team specializes in precision diagnostics for All Industrial Instruments.

Instead of expensive hardware replacements, we provide a cost-effective alternative through certified troubleshooting and part restoration. Our repair process helps you minimize downtime and significantly reduce capital expenditure while ensuring your systems return to peak performance.
