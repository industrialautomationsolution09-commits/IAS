Our Journey

Founded in Pune, Industrial Automation Solution has grown into a trusted partner for global manufacturers. Our approach combines rigorous technical standards with innovative PLC and SCADA problem-solving.

Our Core Solutions

Machine Automation

High-speed logic and motion control for standalone machinery. We specialize in SPM (Special Purpose Machines) logic using high-end PLCs and Servos for precision manufacturing.

Plant & Factory Automation

End-to-end integration of factory floors. We connect multiple production lines to a centralized control system, allowing for seamless data flow and synchronization.

Process Automation

Solutions for continuous process industries. We handle PID loops, flow control, and safety instrumented systems (SIS) for Chemical and Pharma sectors.

Electrical Control Panels

Custom design and fabrication of CP, MCC, PCC, and VFD panels. Built to international standards with rigorous high-voltage testing.

Component Repair

Expert component-level repair for PLCs, HMIs, and VFDs. We help reduce hardware replacement costs with certified troubleshooting.

24/7 Support & AMC

Annual Maintenance Contracts (AMC) designed to ensure zero production loss. Our response team is available for emergency on-site assistance.
